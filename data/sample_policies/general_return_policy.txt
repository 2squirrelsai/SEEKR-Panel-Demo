GENERAL RETURN POLICY

Return Window
Most items purchased from our store can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The item must be in its original condition, unused, and in the original packaging with all tags attached.

Eligibility Requirements
To be eligible for a return:
- The item must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date
- Items must be in new, unused condition
- Original packaging and all tags must be intact
- Include the original receipt or order confirmation

Return Process
1. Log into your account and navigate to Order History
2. Select the order containing the item you wish to return
3. Click "Return Item" and select the reason for return
4. Print the prepaid return shipping label
5. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
6. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location

Refund Timeline
Once we receive your returned item:
- Inspection occurs within 2-3 business days
- Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- Refund will be issued to the original payment method
- You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed

Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Personalized or customized items
- Digital downloads or software
- Opened beauty products or cosmetics
- Perishable food items
- Intimate apparel and swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken)

Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item:
- Contact customer service within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of the damage or defect
- We will arrange a free return pickup
- Replacement or full refund will be issued immediately upon confirmation

Shipping Costs
- Return shipping is free for damaged, defective, or incorrect items
- For standard returns, a $6.99 return shipping fee will be deducted from your refund
- Items returned due to change of mind are subject to return shipping fees

Exchange Policy
We do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size, color, or model:
1. Return the original item for a refund
2. Place a new order for the desired item

This ensures the fastest processing time for your new purchase.
